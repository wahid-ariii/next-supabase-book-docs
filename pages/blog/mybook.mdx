## What is MyBook?

Find books you'll love, and keep track of the books you want to read. Be part of the largest community of book lovers on MyBook

![MyBook Dashboard](/demo.png)

## MyBook can Help You:

- Jog your memory about a book, show, or person on the tip of your tongue
- Find the best book or show to read next
- Empower you to share your entertainment knowledge and opinions with the world’s largest community of fans

## Platform

MyBook is available across a range of devices, including computers, phones, tablets, 
and you can easily transition from one to another.

- See what books your friends are reading.
- Track the books you're reading, have read, and want to read.
- Check out your personalized book recommendations. Our recommendation engine analyzes 20 billion data points to give suggestions tailored to your literary tastes.
- Find out if a book is a good fit for you from our community’s reviews.

<Callout>
  You can also browse through the collections of books, authors, and quotes.
</Callout>

## Our Mission

Our mission is to help people find and share books they love.
