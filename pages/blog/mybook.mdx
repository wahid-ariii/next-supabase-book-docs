## What is MyBook?

Find books you'll love, and keep track of the books you want to read. Be part of the largest community of book lovers on MyBook

![MyBook Dashboard](/demo.png)

## MyBook can Help You:

- Jog your memory about a book, show, or person on the tip of your tongue
- Find the best book or show to watch next
- Empower you to share your entertainment knowledge and opinions with the world’s largest community of fans

## Platform

MyBook is available across a range of devices, including computers, phones, tablets, 
and you can easily transition from one to another.

There are millions of books and show on MyBook. So whether you’re behind the wheel, working out, partying or relaxing, 
the right book or show is always at your fingertips. Choose what you want to watch to, or let MyBook surprise you.

<Callout>
  You can also browse through the collections of books, authors, and quotes.
</Callout>

## Our Mission

Our mission is to unlock the potential of human creativity—by giving a million creative authors 
the opportunity to live off their art and millions of fans the opportunity to enjoy and be inspired by it.
