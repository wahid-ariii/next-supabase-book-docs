## What is MyBook?

MyBook is a digital information and statistics about movies, TV shows as well as actors, directors and other film industry professionals. 
Our searchable database includes millions of movies, TV and entertainment programs and cast and crew members. 

![MyBook Dashboard](/demo.png)

## MyBook can Help You:

- Jog your memory about a movie, show, or person on the tip of your tongue
- Find the best movie or show to watch next
- Empower you to share your entertainment knowledge and opinions with the world’s largest community of fans

## Platform

MyBook is available across a range of devices, including computers, phones, tablets, 
and you can easily transition from one to another.

There are millions of movies and episodes on MyBook. So whether you’re behind the wheel, working out, partying or relaxing, 
the right movie or show is always at your fingertips. Choose what you want to watch to, or let MyBook surprise you.
